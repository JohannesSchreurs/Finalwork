This experience was made for my Final Work assignment.

The goal was to create a visual environment for a song and spoken text by Godspeed You! Black Emperor.

If you are on a mobile network, it is not recommended to proceed, as a lot of data will be downloaded.

Make sure to turn the sound on.

Best results are with a headphone, a Google Cardboard and in landscape mode.

If you have a Google Cardboard, press the Google Cardboard logo in the right bottom corner while the experience is playing.

Start the experience.
